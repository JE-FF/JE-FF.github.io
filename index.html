ABOUT ME

My whole life I've been plugged in and fascinated as computers and the internet changed the world. Naturally, I pursued a career in IT and I became a technician for family entertainment centers where I repaired arcade games and administrated SOHO infrastructure.

I stepped away from IT for a few years to learn more about the world and about myself. I did an Americorps service year, then I worked as a medical assistant and nurse's aide while pursuing nursing informatics as a career that would combine my passions for IT and helping others.
