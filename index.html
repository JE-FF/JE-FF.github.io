ABOUT ME

My whole life I've been plugged in and fascinated as computers and the internet changed the world. Naturally, I pursued a career in technology and I became a technician for family entertainment centers where I worked with audio / visual IT and administrated small office / home office IT systems.

I stepped away from IT for a few years to learn more about the world and about myself. I did an Americorps service year, then I worked as a medical assistant, nurse's aide, and laboratory assistant while pursuing nursing informatics as a career that would combine my passions for IT and helping others.

One year into the Covid-19 pandemic, I left healthcare and decided to pursue a B.S. IT degree instead. I graduated in July 2024, and now I am eagerly looking for a position where I can apply my knowledge as part of a great team where I continue to learn and grow.
